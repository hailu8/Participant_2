Does the per capita income of a country affect the number of medals or limit the number of participants that compete in the summer Olympic Games?

This dashboard focuses on giving insights into the total number of medals won by all athletes and medalists with their corresponding age and gender from 1988 - 2016.. Research shows that higher per capita income implies higher medal totals. You can observe additional insights by looking at each country's income level groups.

How to interact:

  1. The map:
    2. Hover on the map to see country names, and zoom and pan to explore more on the map.
    Click a country on the map, and the butterfly chart will change to reflect the selected country in a darker color.
  2. The Dropdown:
    3. Click on the year dropdown to alternate between the given years.
  3. The butterfly chart:
    4. Hover on each bar to see details.
    Choose "all-athletes" or "all-medalists" using the radio button to explore more.
